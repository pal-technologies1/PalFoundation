About PALFoundation

Empowering communities and transforming lives through compassion, service, and sustainable development.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to create a better and more compassionate world, PALFoundation is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and empowerment initiatives.

Since our inception, we’ve worked with our local partners and volunteers to bring hope to families, supporting vulnerable children, and promoting sustainable growth. Every small act of kindness counts, and together we are making a difference. We have raised over $2 Millon, given out 1500+ Scholarships, dug 320+ Boreholes, Built Classrooms at over 70 schools, given out over 500,000 food packs, organised over 95+ Medical Outreachs and various contributions to the progression of the society

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through education, advocacy, and resource sharing — ensuring that every person has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To build a world where compassion drives action, equality empowers opportunity, and kindness creates lasting change for all.
